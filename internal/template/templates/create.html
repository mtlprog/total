How It Works
  1. Fill in the market details above
  2. Click "Generate Create Transaction" to get the XDR
  3. Copy the XDR and sign it with your Stellar wallet
  4. Submit the signed transaction to the Stellar network
  5. The market account will be created with YES/NO tokens
  6. Users can buy tokens, prices adjust via LMSR
  7. Oracle resolves market when outcome is known
  8. Winners redeem tokens for EURMTL
