Deploy New Market

Deploy a new prediction market via the factory contract. You must sign the transaction with the oracle key.

Step 1: Upload Metadata to IPFS

First, upload your market metadata JSON to IPFS (via Pinata or similar). The metadata should contain: 

{
  "question": "Will BTC reach $100k by end of 2025?",
  "description": "Resolution: Resolves YES if BTC price on Coinbase reaches $100,000 USD at any point before December 31, 2025 23:59 UTC.",
  "resolution_source": "coinbase.com",
  "category": "crypto",
  "end_date": "2025-12-31T23:59:59Z",
  "created_at": "2024-01-01T00:00:00Z"
}

Use Pinata to upload the JSON and get the IPFS hash (CID).

How It Works
  1. Create metadata JSON with question, description, etc.
  2. Upload JSON to IPFS and copy the CID
  3. Fill in the form above with IPFS CID and parameters
  4. Click "Generate Deploy Transaction" to get the XDR
  5. Sign the XDR with your oracle wallet
  6. Submit the signed transaction to Stellar network
  7. New market contract is deployed and registered with factory
  8. Market appears on the markets list automatically
